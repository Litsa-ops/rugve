ABOUT US

RUGVED Systems provides Autonomous Robotics Solutions for the Military.
They are a student run group of highly motivated undergraduate students from
the Manipal Institute of Technology. Diverse group, with majors ranging from
Computer Science Engineering to Electronics, Mechatronics and Mechanical
Engineering shares a common passion for the field of robotics. They also take
part in various Autonomous Robotics Competitions including IGVC (Intelligent
Ground Vehicular Competition) held every year in Michigan, USA.
